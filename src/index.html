Why Choose Liberty Transcripts?

Liberty Transcripts is in the business of providing legal transcription services to courts, attorneys, and court reporting firms across the nation.


As certified electronic transcribers, we have passed the gold standard given by the American Association of Electronic Reporters and Transcribers (AAERT) to produce accurate and high-quality transcripts. To maintain such certification, we have to complete continuing education credits every three years which enables us to stay up-to-date with industry standards and practices as well as stay fresh on education. We must also be a Member In Good Standing with AAERT in order to maintain our certifications.


Rest assured that your transcript request will not be treated as a number. Each transcript request is given due care and attention so that your satisfaction is guaranteed. We are always available via e-mail or telephone and respond promptly to all inquiries or concerns.




How Can I Sign Up?

Liberty Transcripts is approved as a transcription vendor in many federal courts with a specialization in bankruptcy courts. Please review the Bankruptcy Court Jurisdiction Tab above for the full list of courts. If the jurisdiction is listed, Liberty Transcripts can be found on the court website as an approved vendor. Most courts allow YOU to select your vendor of choice. Contact us with the details of your bankruptcy proceeding, and we will take care of the rest.

Liberty Transcripts can also transcribe 341(a) Meeting of Creditors for all bankruptcy court jurisdictions.




What Are Your Page Rates?

Liberty Transcripts adheres to the page rates set forth by the Judicial Conference for all bankruptcy and federal proceedings.
